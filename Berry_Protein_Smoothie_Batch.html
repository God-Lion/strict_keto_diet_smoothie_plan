Berry Protein Smoothie (Single Pot)

About The Recipe

A creamy, low-carb berry smoothie packed with antioxidants, protein, and healthy fats. Perfect for breakfast or post-workout.

When's the best time to have this Keto smoothie?

Enjoy after a workout or as a light breakfast.

How to pick the perfect avocado for this recipe?

Use ripe avocados for best creaminess.

What other ingredients can you add to this Keto-friendly smoothie?

Add almond milk for creaminess or swap cinnamon with vanilla extract.

Prep Time: 5 mins
Serving Size: 1 pot (~30 oz)
Macros (per serving): 4g net carbs, 545 kcal

Ingredients

  • Strawberries, Whole – 50 g
  • Blueberries – 30 g
  • Diced Avocados – 100 g (~½ medium)
  • 100% Whey Isolate Protein Powder, Simply Vanilla – 1 scoop (39 g)
  • Organic Cacao Superfood Powder Net Carbs – 5 g (1 tsp)
  • Cinnamon Powder – ½ tsp
  • Sweetener, Stevia Leaf, Plant-Based – to taste
  • Olive Oil – 1 tbsp
  • Water + ice to fill 30 oz

Instructions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
  4. Serve immediately.
